SH24 instructions kit for men who have sex with men

These pages are provided to help you do the SH24 sexual health test kit. This kit has 4 parts, a blood sample for HIV and Syphilis, a urine sample for Chlamydia and Gonorrhoea, a rectal swab for Gonorrhoea and an Oral swab for Gonorrheoa. Follow the instructions and the test should only take 10 minutes.
