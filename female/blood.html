• Doing the blood sample

    1. Find a clean, flat surface
    2. Lay out the contents of the Blood sample
    3. Open the packaging containing the small yellow topped tube
    4. Remove the yellow top and keep nearby
    5. Place the tube in the packing to help keep it steady
    6. Have some dry clean tissues nearby

  • Wash your hands

    1. Wash your hands with soap and warm water
    2. Warming your hands with warm water really helps when taking the blood sample
    3. Dry your hands thoroughly with a clean towel

  • Sterilising finger tip

    1. Tear open the small packet printed with the words 'Alcho-tip'
    2. The ideal fingers are the second and third finger
    3. Remove the swab and wipe your finger tip

  • Using the lancet

    1. Twist and pull the small purple tip from the lancet
    2. In the opposite hand hold and pinch the lancet between thumb, first and second finger
    3. IMPORTANT Press the tip of the lancet into the tip of the sterile finger firmly and press the button
    4. Discard the lancet it can only be used once

  • Fillind the sample tube

    1. Wipe away the first blood spot
    2. IMPORTANT Stand up with the sample tube on the table
    3. Massage your finger directly above the sample tube allowing blood to drop in
    4. Keep massaging the finger until you have filled the sample tube to the top line
    5. When the sample is full (reaches the 600 line) wipe your finger with a clean tissues and apply a the supplied plaster

  • Replace the lid

    1. Press the yellow lid back onto the sample tube and press firmly closed
    2. Gently rotate the tube a few times to mix the sample

  • Mix the sample

    1. Press the yellow lid back onto the sample tube and press firmly closed
    2. Gently rotate the tube a few times to mix the sample

  • Pack ready for posting

    1. Place the sealed sample tube back in the packaging
    2. Place the completed sample and packaging into the mailing bag provided
