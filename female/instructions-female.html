SH24 instructions kit for women

These pages are provided to help you do the SH24 sexual health test kit. The female kit in made up of two parts. A blood sample that is used to test for HIV and Syphilis and a swab sample that indicates any Gonorrhoea and Chlamydia infections. Both tests are painless, but may cause some slight discomfort. These pages are intended to help you make doing the tests as simple and easy as possible.
