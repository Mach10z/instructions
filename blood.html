  • Doing the blood sample

        Find a clean flat surface
        Lay out the contents of the Blood sample
        Open the packaging containing the small yellow topped tube
        Remove the yellow top and keep nearby
        Place the tube in the packing to help keep it steady
        Have some dry clean tissues to hand

  • Wash your hands

        Wash your hands with soap and warm water
        Warming your hands with warm water really helps when doing the blood sample
        Dry your hands thoroughly with a clean towel

  • Sterilising finger tip

        Tear open the small packet printed with the words 'Alcho-tip'
        The ideal fingers are the middle finger and ring finger
        Remove the swab and wipe your finger tip

  • Using the lancet

        Twist and pull the small purple tip from the lancet
        In the opposite hand hold and pinch the lancet between thumb, first and second finger
        IMPORTANT__ Press the tip of the lancet into the tip of the sterile finger firmly and press the button
        Discard the lancet it can only be used once

  • Fillind the sample tube

        Wipe away the first blood spot
          __IMPORTANT__ Stand up with the sample tube on the table
          Massage your finger directly above the sample tube allowing blood to drop in
          Keep massaging the finger until you have filled the sample tube to the top line
          When the sample is full (reaches the 600 line) wipe your finger with a clean tissues and apply a the supplied plaster

  • Replace the lid

        Press the yellow lid back onto the sample tube and press firmly closed
        Gently rotate the tube a few times to mix the sample

  • Mix the sample

        Press the yellow lid back onto the sample tube and press firmly closed
        Gently rotate the tube a few times to mix the sample

  • Pack ready for posting

        Place the sealed sample tube back in the packaging
        Place the completed sample and packaging into the mailing bag provided
