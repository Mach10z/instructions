SH24 instructions kit for men

These pages are provided to help you do the SH24 sexual health test kit. The kit is in 2 parts, a blood sample for HIV and Syphilis and a urine sampe for Chlamydia and Gonorrhoea. Please follow the instructions closely and it should only take a few minutes to complete and return in the freepost bag provided.
