  • It's best to do this sample in a bathroom. It might be easier by resting one foot on the edge of the bath or toilet to assist with using the swab

  • Open the swab

    1. Twist and pull the purple swab form the container

  • How to use the swab

    1. Carefully insert the soft tip end of the swab into your vagina about 2 inches (5 cm) past the opening of the vagina.
    2. Gently rotate the swab for 10 to 30 seconds

  • Where to take the sample from

    1. Make sure the swab touches the walls of the vagina so that moisture is absorbed by the swab.
